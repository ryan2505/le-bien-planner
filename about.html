A house built on quiet
and the art of detail.

Le Bien Planner Déco is the work of one woman, a curated bench of partners, and nine years of practice in the rooms where celebration becomes memory.

Mme Rosy
Founder & Creative Director

I do not plan events. I compose them.

Rosy began Le Bien in 2017 after a decade spent in hospitality and brand design — a career that taught her how the smallest detail (a fold of linen, the rhythm of a doorman's greeting) decides whether a room feels rented or remembered.

Today she leads every project personally. From the first consultation to the moment your last guest leaves, the eye on the work is hers — supported by a bench of partners chosen, not contracted.

"An event is a piece of theatre that only runs once. My job is to rehearse the things you'll never notice, so that what you do notice is each other."
Mission · Vision · Values

Three quiet principles
guide every project.

We are not a vendor and not an agency. We are an atelier — closer to a maison de couture than a planning firm.

— i. Mission

Compose celebrations, not check lists.

To turn the most important days of your life into a coherent piece of design — emotion, narrative, and detail in one quiet voice.

— ii. Vision

Define quiet luxury in American events.

To bring an editorial, atelier sensibility to a market still defined by spectacle — proving restraint is the highest form of celebration.

— iii. Values

Discretion, devotion, discipline.

Confidentiality with every client. Devotion to the smallest detail. Discipline to deliver, on the day, in silence.

Chronology

A house, built slowly.

Nine years of practice, one project at a time.

2017

The atelier opens

Rosy founds Le Bien in a small studio in Monroeville. First wedding: 42 guests, a barn, and one borrowed candelabra.

2019

The bench is built

Long-term partnerships formed with the florists, calligraphers and culinary teams who remain with us today.

2021

Corporate practice

First gala for a Pittsburgh institution. The corporate event arm is formalised the following spring.

2023

Editorial direction

The maison's signature — ivory, gold, restraint — is published in three regional design titles.

2025

Beyond Pennsylvania

Active production in Philadelphia and New York. The bench grows to forty-two trusted partners.

2026

The next chapter

A private studio in Fox Hill opens to clients by appointment. Couples dance studio launches.
