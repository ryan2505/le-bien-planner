The process

Six steps, from conversation to celebration.

— Step 01

Quiet conversation

A first call or studio visit. We listen — to the story, the guest list, and the things you have not yet put into words.

— Step 02

The brief

A short editorial document — the narrative, the palette, the rhythm. The thing we will design against.

— Step 03

Atelier proposal

Venues, partners, design directions and a transparent budget — composed into one bound proposal.

— Step 04

Composition

Months of quiet work. Sourcing, sampling, tasting, fitting, refining. You see what matters; we hold the rest.

— Step 05

Rehearsal

The week before. Walk-throughs, dress rehearsal, room set, lighting cues. Nothing left to the day.

— Step 06

The day

We are present, we are quiet, and we are everywhere. You spend the day with each other.
